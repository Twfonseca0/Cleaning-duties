CLEANING CAPTAIN

Instructions:

  • 1. Enter the name of the worker in the text box for the respective day. 2. Click the "Add Worker" button to add the worker's name to the list. 3. Repeat steps 1 and 2 for all workers of the shift for that day. 4. After entering all names, click the "Sort" button to randomly assign a worker. 5. The randomly selected worker's name will be displayed below the buttons.

    • Monday

    Hood vents: remove from the line, in the dish area carefully scrub, run through the dishwasher and let dry before putting it back.

    Tuesday

    Dry storage: clean, sanitize and organize shelving, sweep and mop. Wipe downstairs shelving.

    Wednesday

    Behind equipment: pull equipment forward, sweep and mop the floor. Degrease as necessary.

    Thursday

    Dishpit: pull out supplies from under sink, mop, wipe walls and clean top of dishwasher.

    Friday

    Ovens: Degrease doors, interiors. Remove shelves, soak in degreaser, scrub and clean.

    Saturday

    Garbage Cans: remove any food residue from inside and sides of the cans. Bleach inside out, clean with water and let it dry.

    Sunday

    Prep-fridge: clean and organize prep fridge. Change the bottles container if necessary.
